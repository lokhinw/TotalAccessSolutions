WHO ARE WE?

Total Access Solutions provides all that you need for independence. Over 30 years of experience, we have the creativity to help you find a barrier free solution that works for you and your family.  We understand that your needs are unique to you, and we are committed to providing the most cost effective, quality safe products designed to ensure you receive the solution best suited for you. Our core value is to be your one stop location for all your access needs, our company provides a unique collection of home access modifications including, ramps, chair lifts, grab bar installation, door openers, overhead ceiling lift systems, vehicle lifts and more. We would be happy to provide you with a in home assessment.
